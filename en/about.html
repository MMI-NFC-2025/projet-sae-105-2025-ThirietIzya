ABOUT

This site was created as part of a project of the BUT MMI de Montbéliard it explores the effet Mathilda.

Through visual and documentary content, it aims to make the invisible visible and to rehabilitate these forgotten figures.

The project highlights Katherine Johnson, a NASA mathematician whose calculations were essential to American space missions, but whose contribution has long remained in the shadows.
