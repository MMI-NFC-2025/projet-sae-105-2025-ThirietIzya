CONTACT

Contact (1997), directed by Robert Zemeckis and adapted from the novel by Carl Sagan, tells the story of Ellie Arroway, a brilliant astrophysicist played by Jodie Foster, who captures an extraterrestrial signal proving the existence of life beyond Earth. The film explores both the scientific quest and the struggle of a woman to be recognized in an environment dominated by men. Through this story combining science, faith and perseverance, Contact highlights the fight for the legitimacy of women researchers and the power of human curiosity facing the unknown.
