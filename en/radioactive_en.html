RADIOACTIVE

Radioactive (2019), directed by Marjane Satrapi, traces the exceptional life of Marie Curie, a pioneering scientist and the first woman to have received a Nobel Prize. The film explores both her groundbreaking findings on radioactivity and the personal challenges she faced in a male-dominated society. With a staging that is both poetic and powerful, Radioactive pays tribute to a visionary woman whose genius revolutionized science and paved the way for generations of researchers.
